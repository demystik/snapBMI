Simple & Clear Explanation: BMI Calculator

What is BMI?
BMI stands for Body Mass Index.  

It’s a quick and simple way to check if your weight is healthy for your height. 
It helps estimate whether you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

The Logic Behind BMI

The formula is very straightforward:



 BMI Categories (What the number means)

| BMI Range          | Category          | Meaning                          |
|--------------------|-------------------|----------------------------------|
| Below 18.5         | Underweight       | You may need to gain weight      |
| 18.5 – 24.9        | Normal / Healthy  | Good range!                      |
| 25.0 – 29.9        | Overweight        | You should consider losing weight|
| 30.0 and above     | Obese             | High risk for health problems    |


- BMI is not perfect — it doesn’t distinguish between fat and muscle.
- A bodybuilder with lots of muscle may have high BMI but low body fat.
- An older person with low muscle may have normal BMI but high body fat.

- Still, it’s the most commonly used simple health indicator worldwide.

How a BMI Calculator Usually Works (Logic Flow)

1. User enters weight (in kg or pounds)
2. User enters height (in cm, meters, or feet/inches)
3. App converts everything to kg and meters
4. Calculates: BMI = weight / (height  height)
5. Shows the BMI number
6. Shows the category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese)
7. Sometimes gives a short health message
